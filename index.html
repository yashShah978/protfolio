Hi, I’m Yash Shah

"I am Yash Shah, currently pursuing my ICT Diploma in Semester 5 at Government Polytechnic Gandhinagar. I am also interning at Tinkering India, where I have the opportunity to apply my skills in technology and innovation while contributing to meaningful projects. During my internship, I have been actively involved in various aspects of the organization's initiatives, including supporting the development of STEM kits and educational tools, as well as assisting in the growth of Tinkering India’s mission to foster creativity and critical thinking among students.
